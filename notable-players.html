Famous Pirates Players Throughout History

The Pittsburgh Pirates historically have had some of the best players to ever play the game on their team, to list a few...

Roberto Clemente

Roberto was picked up by the Pirates in 1955 in the rule 5 draft from the dodgers, he blossomed into one of the best players of all time while leading the Pirates to 2 World Series Championships, finishing with 3000 hits perfectly before tragically dying in a plane crash on a charity mission to Nicaragua.

Willie Stargell

Originally came up with the Pirates in 1962, Willie is one of the best and most beloved Pirates players ever, he is mostly known for his power, and the longest home run in Dodger Stadium ever.

Barry Bonds

Drafted by the Pirates originally, he is most known for his tenure with the San Francisco Giants, he won MVP with the Pirates in 1992 and eventually became the Home Run King with 755 all time home runs, passing Hank Aaron.

Honus Wagner

The first Pirates great, he is considered one of the greatest to ever play the game, he is often overshadowed by other platyers of his time like Ty Cobb, but he was one of the best offensive and defensive shortstops of all time, playing for the Pirates from 1899-1917.

Andrew McCutchen

Andrew McCutchen was drafted by the Pirates in the 1st round in 2005, and came up with them in 2009. He is one of the most beloved Pirates players ever, loved by fans for his kindness and skill of the game. In 2013 he won NL MVP while leading the Pirates to their first playoff birth in over 20 years. He left the Pirates in 2018, but returned in 2023 and is now here to stay.

Dave Parker

Also known as "The Cobra", Dave Parker was recently inducted into baseballs hall of fame, and is known for his insane power and arm strength.

The 1970's:

During the 1970's the Pittsburgh Pirates were at their all time peak, they had stars such as Roberto Clemente, WIllie Stargell, and other lesser known players like Steve Blass.

The 1971 World Series

The Pirates were back in the World Series in 1971, this time against the Orioles, they won the series in game 7, thanks to stars like Roberto Clemente, Steve Blass, and Willie Stargell.

The 1979 World Series

The Pirates made it to the World Series once again in 1979, against the Baltimore Orioles for a second time. The Orioles were winning the series 3 games to 1, but the Pirates came back and won three consecutive games to take the series in game 7, led by Willie Stargell, the Co NL MVP that year (tied with Keith Hernandez).
